Guacamole

An avacado based dip

This dish filled with avacados, onions, tomatoes, garlic, and cilantro is great for any fiesta!

Ingredients:

  • Avacados
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Slice the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork and make it as chunky or smooth as youâ€™d like.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and stir together. Give it a taste test and add a pinch more salt or lime juice if needed.
  4. Serve the guacamole with tortilla chips.
