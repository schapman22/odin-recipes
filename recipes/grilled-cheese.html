Grilled Cheese

A classic hot sandwhich!

This cheesy delicous treat can be a perfect easy lunch

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Butter two slices of bread.
  2. Add cheese in between both slices.
  3. Fry on fryinging pan until golden brown.
  4. Flip and do same on other side.
