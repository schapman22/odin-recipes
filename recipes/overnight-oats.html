Overnight Oats

An oatmeal dish good hot or cold!

This delicous snack is a simple oatmeal made without cooking.

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Honey
  • Nuts
  • Toppings of choice

Steps:

  1. Combine oats and mile in a mason jar.
  2. Add honey and nuts.
  3. Add any other toppings you like.
  4. Store in fridge overnight.
